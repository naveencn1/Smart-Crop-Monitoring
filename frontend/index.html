    About the System

    Our platform integrates Artificial Intelligence with Edge Computing to provide offline-capable, real-time farm diagnostics. IoT sensors embedded in fields collect vital data, while aerial drone monitoring highlights stressed areas. On-site AI models analyze leaf images directly, recommending instant treatments without requiring high-bandwidth internet.
